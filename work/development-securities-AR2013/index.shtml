Overview

Annual Report 2013

For Development Securities' Annual Report 2013 we wanted to show their property portfolio in context within the city. We commissioned photographer Jason Hawkes to document the properties from the air, shooting from macro to micro in scale. We designed the print report on the idea of building blocks. My challenge was to translate this idea online.
