About me

Hello

I am Enora, a designer and art director with a passion for solving problems with ideas.

Overview

Over the past five years I have gained experience in digital design and art direction for films, photography and illustration. Before that I tried my hand at letterpress and worked as a designer for the cultural industries.

Born in France, I settled in London since 2009 after having visited a few other countries. I have a passion for discovering new cultures and people which has led me to speak 5 languages fluently. Have a look at my LinkedIn profile to know more.
